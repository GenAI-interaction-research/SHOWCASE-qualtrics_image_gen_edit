Text Inpainting:

  1. Click "Draw Selection" to start selecting an area
  2. Click and drag your mouse to draw around the area you want to modify
  3. You can make multiple selections - each area will be included
  4. Use "Clear Selection" to remove all selections
  5. Type what you want to appear in the selected areas
  6. Click "Apply Changes" to modify the selected areas

Tip: Be as specific as possible in your text prompt!

Cleanup:

  1. Click "Draw Selection" to start selecting an area
  2. Click and drag your mouse to draw around the areas you want to remove
  3. You can make multiple selections to remove multiple areas
  4. Use "Clear Selection" to start over
  5. Click "Apply Changes" to remove the selected areas

Note: Always uses high-quality processing

Reimagine:

  1. Describe how you want to transform the entire image
  2. No selection needed - affects the whole picture
  3. Click "Apply Changes" to reimagine

Example: "Make it cyberpunk style"
